Into the Dungeon

Multi-player game to play online or sharing a single device

  1. Pick the hero who will enter the dungeon
  2. Fill the dungeon with monsters
  3. Remove pieces of the hero's equipment
  4. Run away or dare to enter the dungeon
  5. Survive two dungeons or get other players killed

This game is a prototype: Find out more about its current state and expected improvements.

The design fits only mobile devices for now: if your screen is larger, use your browser's dev tools to set the viewport size at 360x640px.
